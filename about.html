About Us

About Us

Melo Pixels Ltd is a software development outsourcing company. Our core business is to establish and service fully managed dedicated software development team in Bangladesh, as well as deliver custom software development projects for our customers world-wide.

We, unlike robots, do not work only with our brains. We are humans with an uncanny love for technology and immense love for solutions. Passion for the work we do continuously inspire us to be more and more involved. Adapting to changes help us keep up with latest trends.

We understand that no one wants to start an information technology outsourcing project facing a lot of unknowns or unspecified cost projections. Therefore our offshore software development team provides all our clients with preliminary estimates for the digital projects.

We have been providing custom software development services to lots of different companies in USA, Canada, UK, Germany and other countries. No matter where you are: in New York, Denver or Ontario, or in London, Berlin or Helsinki, Melo Pixels is always happy to be at hand for you.

We started our journey as a Freelancing team in Elance, latter we converted to a company. We are a registered company in UK and Bangladesh.
